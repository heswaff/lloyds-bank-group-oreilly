Academies

Structured pathways for organisation-wide rollout.

Beyond the headline catalogue volume, two Academies bundle curated content from across the platform into sequenced learning paths suitable for non-specialist audiences.

AI Academy
Eight learning paths

Designed to take learners from novice through to incorporating AI into everyday tasks. The objective is to build foundational AI skills that boost enterprise-wide productivity, empowering roles in business analysis, marketing, sales, product management, HR, and leadership to work more efficiently.

  • GenAI Essentials for Everyone
  • GenAI for Leaders
  • GenAI for Project Management
  • GenAI for Executives
  • GenAI for Marketing
  • GenAI for HR
  • GenAI for Business Analysis
  • GenAI for Product Management
Data Literacy Academy
Five learning paths

Recently released on the platform. Designed to build foundational data literacy with structured learning paths – building the skills to confidently source, manage, engage with, and communicate data to make informed decisions across the business.

  • Data Essentials for Everyone
  • Data-Informed Decision Making
  • Data Workflows with Everyday Tools
  • Data Storytelling and Communication
  • Data Logic for Business Systems
Modalities

Plain-language definitions.

A glossary of every content modality referenced in this response.

ModalityDefinition
BooksFull-text technical and business books from O’Reilly and over 200 partner publishers, including early-release titles.
On-demand video coursesPre-recorded video courses with structured curricula, taught by industry practitioners and authors.
Live eventsInteractive instructor-led sessions, including live courses, expert-led conversations, Katas challenges, and Superstream events.
Hands-on labsBrowser-based, sandboxed environments for practising real-world skills (cloud platforms, languages, tools) without local setup.
Certification practice testsTimed, scored practice exams aligned to industry certifications (AWS, Azure, GCP, CISSP, PMP, and others).
Certification guidesStructured study guides aligned to specific certification exam requirements.
Expert playlistsCurated learning sequences combining books, videos, and labs, assembled by O’Reilly experts on a focused skill or technology area.
AudiobooksAudio editions of selected technical and business titles.
Skill plansSkill-based assessments that produce a verifiable, shareable digital credential on completion.
AcademiesEnterprise-grade structured programs with learning paths designed for organization-wide rollout.
